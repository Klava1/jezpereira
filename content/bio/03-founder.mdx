Beyond the decks, Jez co-founded the London-based DJ agency *Syck Talent* in 2015, supplying DJs to prestigious clients including Annabel's, KOKO, Gordon Ramsay Restaurants UK & Miami, and other luxury venues worldwide.

He also created *LOST IN MUSYCK* — a platform dedicated to showcasing emerging DJs — and is now launching *SYCK RECORDS* — building a creative ecosystem designed to support, develop, and empower the next generation of artists.

For Jez, music has always been about feeling, connection, emotion, and creating unforgettable experiences — a philosophy captured perfectly in his message:

> Let the MUSYCK do the talking.
