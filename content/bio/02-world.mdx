Over the years, Jez has shared stages with world-renowned artists including Black Coffee, &ME, David Guetta and many more.

His global journey has taken him from Burning Man to Tao, Las Vegas, from collaborations with PACHA Ibiza, PACHA Italy, and Pacha Buzios, Brazil, to performances at Armani Privé Dubai — alongside standalone events across New York, Colombia, and for Redbull in Japan.

*All independently. Without the backing of a manager or agent.*
