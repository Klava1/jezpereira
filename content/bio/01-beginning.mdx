Jez Pereira is a globally recognised DJ and producer originally from Bombay, India, where his musical journey began in 1993 at the city's legendary venues — including Piano Bar and China Garden.

In 2001, he moved to London and immersed himself in the UK underground dance scene, drawing inspiration from iconic clubs such as KOKO and Ministry of Sound, where his distinctive *freestyle* sound and musical identity evolved.
